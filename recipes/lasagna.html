Lasagna

Description

A chicken taco twist on the classic lasagna.

Per Serving: 271 calories; protein 19.9g; carbohydrates 21.6g; fat 11.4g; cholesterol 56.7mg; sodium 904.5mg.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 2 (1 ounce) packets taco seasoning mix (such as Old El Paso
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, lime juice, and taco seasoning in a large bowl. Stir to make sure chicken is evenly combined
  2. Cook chicken mixture over medium heat until firm, about 15 minutes.
  3. Mix 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, sour cream, and salsa in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Put a few spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture in the bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish. Layer noodles and chicken to top. Cover with remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake int the preheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese is melted, about 1 hour.
